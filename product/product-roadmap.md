# Product Roadmap & Sections

1. **Onboarding & Authentication**
   - Continue with Phone, Google, or Apple.
   - Initial profile setup and syncing contacts to build the "Tribe".
2. **Home (Dashboard / Discover)**
   - A contextual feed of recent activity and recommendations from your network.
   - Search functionality to find specific services (e.g., "plumber").
   - Highlighted "Curated Deals" exclusive to the user's Tribe.
3. **Provider Profile & Insights**
   - Detailed view of a Service Provider.
   - Displays the aggregated "Trust Score" and breakdown (Budget Friendly, Efficiency, Quality, On-time Reliability).
   - Shows exactly *who* in your network recommended them.
4. **Add Recommendation**
   - A simple flow for a user to add a new trusted service provider to their network.
   - Includes inputting ratings across the core metrics.
5. **My Tribe (Network)**
   - Managing personal connections, seeing who is in your network, and exploring beyond your immediate circle if needed.
6. **User Profile & Settings**
   - Managing personal details, viewing past recommendations given, and app preferences.
