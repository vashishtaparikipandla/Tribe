# Product Overview: Tribe

## Product Name
Tribe

## Description
A trusted, review-based service provider network built exclusively on recommendations from your personal contacts and connections.

## The Problem
The modern generation finds it difficult and anxiety-inducing to find reliable service providers (plumbers, doctors, contractors). Endless platforms provide infinite choices, but lack genuine trust. People dislike the hassle of bargaining and negotiation, preferring digital, transparent, and trusted sources.

## The Solution
Tribe digitizes the "word of mouth" experience. Instead of relying on anonymous reviews, users see recommendations only from their actual network ("small circles"). It eliminates negotiation friction by promoting transparent pricing, encourages repeat orders, and provides decision-ready insights (Trust Scores) to make hiring a service provider simple and safe.
